Honoring Classmates Who Have Transitioned

We honor and remember our classmates who have transitioned.

Their memories live on in our hearts and in the bonds we shared at Richmond Senior High.

🕯️ Memorial Moment

During our Saturday night Great Gatsby Party, we will observe a special moment of silence from 7:30 – 7:45 PM to honor our classmates who have transitioned.

This will be a time for reflection, remembrance, and celebrating the lives of those who touched our hearts during our time together.

Frequently Asked Questions

💰 What are the early bird ticket prices?

Single Class Member: $35 (Early Bird – April 25 – May 31, 2026)

Couples Ticket: $60 (Early Bird – April 25 – May 31, 2026) – Two class members or member + spouse

Non-Class Member / Guest: $30 – For guests who did not graduate with us

Additional Guests: $30 per person for Saturday Night event (21 and up)

All Saturday night tickets include admission to the Great Gatsby Party and entertainment. All other activities are pay as you go.

📍 Where is the Saturday night party?

The Great Gatsby Party will be held at Leak Street Cultural Center on Saturday, October 3, 2026, from 7:00 PM to 12:00 AM. 35 and up only.

🎭 What should I wear to the evening celebration?

Come dressed in Gatsby theme or whatever you desire! Think flapper dresses, feathers, pearls, suspenders, bow ties, and vintage glamour—or wear whatever makes you feel fabulous. Don't let attire stop you from joining us! Prizes will be awarded for best dressed!

🎊 Tell me about Friday's events

Join us for the Class of 1991 Parade down main street in Rockingham, led by Toni Roscoe Maples, followed by the Friday night football game at Richmond Senior High School Stadium starting at 7:00 PM. Pick up your reunion packets at the gate table and reconnect during halftime!

🤝 What are the community service projects?

Saturday morning and afternoon we'll be giving back to our community with service projects (9 AM–12 PM, led by Calandra McDonald) and a backpack giveaway for local students (1 PM–4 PM, led by Chewuna Patterson). Specific locations will be updated as confirmed.

💳 What deposit is required?

A $25 nonrefundable deposit is required to secure your RSVP for Saturday events. This deposit will be applied to your Saturday event ticket purchase or transferred to purchase your class reunion t-shirt. If you would like to donate to pay for a classmate, please indicate that in the memo. Send via Cash App ($classof1991reunion) or Venmo (@raiderclassreunion91), and include your full name in the payment memo.

🍳 Tell me about Sunday brunch

Our final gathering will be Sunday brunch from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM on October 4. Enjoy food, fellowship, and words of inspiration from a special guest speaker. It's a relaxed way to say goodbye and share final memories before everyone heads home.

📧 How do I contact the reunion committee?

Email us at 91raidercommittee@gmail.com with any questions or concerns.
